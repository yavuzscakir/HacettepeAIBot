Hacettepe AI Assistant

An AI Powered Legislative Chatbot

Why Is This Project Truly Needed?

Students often have a wide range of questions regarding university procedures and policies. These inquiries are directed through visits and emails, increasing the administrative workload. The AI Assistant enables students to quickly access information while reducing the burden on staff.

How Does It Work?

A dataset based on Hacettepe University regulations was prepared in JSON format, vectorized and indexed with FAISS. User queries are matched with the most relevant articles using semantic search and re-ranked using a cross-encoder. Finally, a language model generates context-aware answers from the top results.

Flexible & Customizable

The system can be customized to fit the workflows of different departments, offering a user-friendly interface and 24/7 access to complex academic regulations through natural language queries.
