Why Is This Project Truly Needed?

University secretaries receive hundreds of procedural and academic queries from students every week. These are handled through emails and physical visits, significantly increasing the administrative burden. The AI Assistant provides a fast and reliable self-service option, enabling students to access official information instantly — while reducing workload on staff.

What Does This Assistant Offer?

This AI-powered assistant provides instant answers to students’ regulation-related questions using natural language understanding. It operates 24/7, reduces unnecessary email traffic, and saves valuable time during busy academic periods — making it a practical self-service tool for both students and university staff.

Benefits for the Students

The AI Assistant allows students to get instant answers to complex academic regulation questions without waiting in lines or sending emails. This provides clarity and confidence, especially during exam periods or while planning course selections.

It empowers students to access information anytime, from any device, reducing dependency on administrative staff and enabling more independent decision-making throughout their university journey.

Benefits for the University

By automating the process of answering regulation-related questions, the assistant significantly reduces the workload on academic and administrative staff. This results in faster, more consistent communication with students while allowing secretaries and faculty to focus on more complex tasks. The system also ensures students receive standardized, accurate information — minimizing misunderstandings and repeat inquiries.

Furthermore, usage analytics collected anonymously can offer valuable insights into student needs, helping departments identify common areas of confusion and improve future policies or documentation.

How Does It Work?



JSON dataset prepared from Hacettepe University regulations.
➜


Data vectorized & indexed with FAISS for search.
➜


Questions matched with most relevant entries.
➜


Results re-ranked using cross-encoder.
➜


LLM generates final answer from top-ranked entries.
